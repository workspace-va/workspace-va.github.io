THE REMOTE ADVANTAGE: ERGONOMICS FOR A WORK-LIFE BALANCE

"Hi, I’m Jesson Famentera. At 28 years old, I’ve already worn many hats—from proud dog owner to devoted father. My three dogs—Everette, Finley, and Revo—keep me on my toes with their playful antics. But it’s my two incredible daughters, Fjolla and Fortnne, who truly light up my world. As a father, I’ve learned patience, empathy, and the art of bedtime storytelling. Whether I’m managing spreadsheets at work or building pillow forts at home, I embrace each role with enthusiasm. Life is a beautiful juggling act, and I’m grateful for every moment."

Family Photo during busy work week



While my days are often filled with digital tasks and spreadsheets, my heart finds solace in the natural world. Living in a remote region of Taguig offers a unique escape from the hustle and bustle. Here, I've cultivated a vibrant garden bursting with life. From juicy tomatoes to flourishing grape cuttings, I find immense satisfaction in nurturing these plants and watching them thrive. Tending to my garden allows me to connect with nature and provides a sense of calm amidst the busyness of life.

My taste in music reflects the diverse aspects of my personality. While working on coding projects or tackling complex business challenges, I often find myself drawn to the soothing melodies of light music. The calming instrumentals help me focus and maintain a clear headspace. Yet, when it's time to unwind and de-stress, the nostalgic beats of 2000s R&B transport me back to a simpler time. This genre's vibrant energy brings a smile to my face and reminds me to embrace the joy of life.

My professional journey has been a dynamic blend of learning and experience. My initial foray into bookkeeping equipped me with a strong foundation in financial management. However, my thirst for knowledge and a desire to build things from scratch led me down the path of software development. Now, I possess a well-rounded skillset that bridges the gap between business management and full-stack development. This multifaceted approach allows me to tackle challenges from different perspectives and create effective solutions.

I believe that learning is a lifelong journey. Whether it's mastering a new coding language, exploring a complex financial concept, or simply understanding the intricate needs of my garden, I'm always seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge. This constant learning fuels my growth and allows me to approach every aspect of life with a fresh perspective.

Despite the demands of my work and personal life, family remains my top priority. Every moment spent with my daughters, Fjolla and Fortnne, is a treasure. They are my source of endless joy and inspiration. Fatherhood has instilled in me invaluable life lessons: patience, empathy, and the importance of fostering a child's imagination through bedtime stories and playful adventures.

Life throws its fair share of curveballs, and I believe that challenges are opportunities for growth. My ability to juggle various roles – entrepreneur, developer, father, and nature enthusiast – allows me to approach each obstacle with a resourceful and adaptable mindset. I find satisfaction in overcoming hurdles and emerging stronger on the other side.

Looking ahead, I'm filled with excitement for the possibilities that lie ahead. I'm dedicated to continuously honing my skills, both professionally and personally. My goal is to strike a perfect balance between nurturing my family, contributing meaningfully to the world of technology, and maintaining my connection with the natural world.

I'm always interested in connecting with like-minded individuals who share a passion for learning, technology, and creating a positive impact. Feel free to reach out and say hello!
